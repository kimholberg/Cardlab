This is CardLab

CardLab has been a leader in advanced electronic smartcards, developing patented technologies in microelectronics, biometrics, and secure identity solutions. Through years of research and innovation, we have built a strong foundation in high-security authentication, shaping the future of access control.

With this expertise, we are now bringing a new range of cost-effective, ready-to-deploy biometric access cards to market. These solutions leverage our deep knowledge of secure identity and authentication, offering strong security, ease of use, and compatibility with existing infrastructure. Our goal is to make high-security access control more accessible and scalable for businesses, governments, and critical infrastructure operators.

CardLab is headquartered in Greater Copenhagen, Denmark, with a dedicated software development team, QuardLock, in Aalborg, Denmark. Our high-quality manufacturing facility in Bangkok, Thailand ensures precision, scalability, and cost-efficiency, allowing us to meet global demand with consistent quality and reliability.
