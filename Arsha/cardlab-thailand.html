CardLab Thailand

Your Trusted Partner in High-Quality Smartcard Production

CardLab Thailand Co., Ltd. operates a cutting-edge manufacturing facility located in the Tong Grow Industrial Park in Chonburi Province, just 45 minutes from Bangkok's Suvarnabhumi International Airport. This strategic location ensures efficient logistics and timely delivery to customers worldwide. Specializing in advanced smart card production, our facility supports a variety of RFID inlays, including UHF, HF, and LF technologies. Whether your needs include unique inlay/prelam formats or ISO-standard cards with specific chip requirements, our team is ready to deliver precision, reliability, and scalability.

At CardLab Thailand, we take a comprehensive approach to material sourcing and production. From premium plastics, adhesives, and foils to advanced printing inks and secure packaging solutions, every component meets rigorous quality standards. By leveraging our partnerships with standardized laboratories, we ensure that all materials are carefully tested, controlled, and approved for use in smart card production..

Our experienced team focuses on reducing time-to-market through efficient product development processes. By fine-tuning production lines, integrating specialized machinery, and offering first-level technical support, we help customers seamlessly introduce new products. Furthermore, our consultancy services address key challenges such as improving production capacity, reducing yield loss, and optimizing processes with new materials and equipment.

At every step, quality is our priority. Products undergo functional and visual inspections, while clients benefit from full technical support. With a proven track record of delivering reliable and secure solutions, CardLab Thailand combines global reach with localized expertise to serve clients across North America, Europe, and Asia.
