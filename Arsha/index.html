Passwordless Biometric Identification

CardLab provides reliable and efficient access control by eliminating the need for traditional keys and passwords. Our biometric authentication cards use your fingerprint for secure access, making access management simpler and more secure. Designed to work with existing systems, CardLab offers a straightforward, hassle-free solution for enterprises, government institutions, and critical infrastructure operators.

Offline - Not Online !

Offline verification with card-based credentials enhances security, privacy, and reliability by keeping data stored directly on the card. This minimizes cyber risks and works well to provide quick and secure authentication without needing internet access.

Tokenization means high security

Tokenization enhances security by replacing sensitive data with tokens, reducing the risk of breaches. GDPR and PCI DSS is another benefit, while minimizing fraud since tokens can’t be easily decoded. Tokenization simplifies data management, builds customer trust, and is flexible for various uses beyond payments. On top you should consider added benefits reduced costs due to lower breach risks, and smoother transactions across platforms. At CardLab we offer a tokenization option on our high-end solutions.
